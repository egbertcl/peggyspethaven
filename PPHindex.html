SERVICES

I care for dogs one year or older who are 35 pounds or less and are spayed/neutered. So that everyone is safe, we care for non-aggressive and crate trained dogs. All dogs need to be current on their rabies shot.

DAYCARE AT PEGGY'S PET HAVEN

  • From 7 am to 6:30 pm
  • I can host up to 2 dogs per day that will not be staying overnight.
  • Potty breaks every 2-4 hours.
  • Playtime, walks, naps and visits to the dog park (if allowed).

Rates

  • $25 a day
  • $50 for puppies 3-9 months
  • 25% discount for 1st additional dog
  • 50% discount for 2nd additional dog

OVERNIGHT AT PEGGY'S DAYCARE

  • Potty breaks every 2-4 hours during the 24 hour period.
  • Playtime, walks, naps and visits to the dog park (if allowed).
  • I choose to host only 4 dogs per overnight period because this allows me to give your pet the individual attention they deserve.

Rates

  • $35 a night
  • $50 for puppies 3-9 months
  • 25% discount for 1st additional dog
  • 50% discount for 2nd additional dog
